Color System

Cream forward. Navy anchored.

Warm Off-White is the default surface across most touchpoints. Midnight Navy anchors hero moments, footers, and any single-focus brand surface. Accent Orange drives every CTA. No teal. No blue gradients. WCAG 2.2 AA compliant across approved pairings.

Rules

Color do's and don'ts

Do

  • Use Warm Off-White (#F5F3F0) as the default surface across most pages, posts, and ads
  • Use Midnight Navy for hero moments, footers, and accent sections
  • Reserve Accent Orange for CTAs, key highlights, and the brand rule
  • Use Orange Button (#B84A08) for filled buttons to meet WCAG AA
  • Pair navy text with cream surfaces for body copy; reverse only on dark sections
  • Use Success Green (#5DD68A) instead of teal for any positive state

Don't

  • Use teal anywhere · ever
  • Use blue gradients or multi-color gradients
  • Use orange backgrounds behind orange text
  • Recolor the logo into accent colors or non-brand hues
  • Use pure black (#000) or pure white (#FFF) on brand surfaces
